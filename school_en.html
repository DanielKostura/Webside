School

The Gymnasium of Ján Adam Rayman in Prešov is a prestigious high school with a long and rich history. This gymnasium is one of the most significant educational institutions in the eastern part of Slovakia, offering students an excellent opportunity to receive a quality education and prepare for their future careers. It was founded in 1949 and has since provided excellent education to students from Prešov and the surrounding areas. The Gymnasium of Ján Adam Rayman offers students a choice from a wide range of subjects and provides a strong academic program. Students have the opportunity to delve deeper into subjects that interest them and prepare for their final exams and further higher education. In addition to academic education, the school places an emphasis on the personal development of its students. It supports various cultural and sports activities, helping students develop their interests and gain important life experiences. The Gymnasium of Ján Adam Rayman in Prešov is a place where young people can realize their potential, discover their passions, and prepare for a successful future. It is an institution with a rich tradition and the pride of eastern Slovakia, continuing to provide excellent education for future generations.
