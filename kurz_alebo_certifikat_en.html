Correspondence Programming Seminar

The seminar is organized by Comenius University in Bratislava, and I have been participating in it since December 2020.
Typically, solutions for this seminar consist of a functional program that the website automatically tests and a textual description of what the program does.

  • CPS will improve your algorithmic thinking and programming skills
  • Many CPS participants have been later employed by companies like Google, Facebook, and Eset
  • Others now teach at universities and engage in scientific research

Spring CyberSecurityDay 2023

The lecture is organized by the Faculty of Science, Pavol Jozef Šafárik University in Košice, and I attended it in April 2023.
The lecture focused in more detail on the issues of security threats with an emphasis on online services and online games. During the workshop, experts introduced us to open-source intelligence analysis (OSINT analysis), forensic analysis of operational memory, and live forensic analysis.
