My Studies at School

Since 2016, I have been a student at Gymnasium Jana Adam Rayman. I am studying at a secondary school with excellent academic performance and no behavior problems. During my studies, I participate in competitions such as Young European, Matboj, and Náboj, as well as knowledge Olympiads in mathematics, geography, and computer science. I also represent the school in sports competitions like handball, basketball, and chess.


About the School

The Gymnasium Jana Adam Rayman in Prešov is a prestigious secondary school with a long and rich history. This gymnasium is one of the most significant educational institutions in the eastern part of Slovakia and offers students an excellent opportunity to receive a quality education and prepare for their future careers. It was founded in 1949 and has been providing excellent education for students from Prešov and the surrounding villages since then. Gymnasium Jana Adam Rayman offers students a choice of a wide range of subjects and provides a strong academic program. Students have the opportunity to delve deeper into subjects that interest them and prepare for final exams and further higher education. In addition to academic education, the school places emphasis on the personal development of students. It is an institution with a rich tradition and a source of pride for Eastern Slovakia, continuing to provide excellent education for future generations.
